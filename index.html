Shaping Tomorrow's

A student-centered Professor at Lovely Professional University with expertise in Biometrics and Linux, bringing 14+ years of academic excellence and research innovation.

I am passionate about bridging the gap between academic research and real-world problem solving — from touch screen authentication systems to COVID-19 detection using deep learning.
